Mark Your Way

Ever thought about keeping track of your sightseeing? Well with our web app Mark Your Way, you can! We tried making an interactive web app into a game which allows the user to keep track of the landmarks he visits in the city of Lisbon. This website will ask you a few questions to ensure you have been to those landmarks and will award you points according to the correct answers you get. Try it now!
