Portugal Lisbon

Landmark Name
Lisbon is the Capital of Portugal, the country with the oldest borders of whole Europe. Built on top of seven hills, it is well known for its story-rich avenues and breath-taking pathways along the tight roads in Alfama. It is a culturally rich capital and started as a settlement in 1200BCE. Being the departure place for the Portuguese discoveries, Lisbon has kept and adapted the several cultures it has been exposed to. It has even been said to have welcome Ulysses during his travels. It is a definitely a city worth getting lost in.

5. You can see your total points (shown on the bottom right of the map) increse each time you get points.
